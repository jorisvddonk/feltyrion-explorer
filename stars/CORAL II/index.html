CLOUDS, 5 ARE VERY LARGE, SUBSTELLAR OBJECT, NOT CONSISTENT, AND THE
OTHER FOUR ARE SMALL, SOLID, DUSTY, CRATERIZED, NO ATMOSPHERE. SUCH
ARE THE WONDERS OF NATURE. SKINNYMON EPOC 6011 582.45
