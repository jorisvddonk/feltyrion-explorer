Guide entries:
A TRINARY SYSTEM, SOME 20-30LY FROM FARHOME. ALL THREE STARS ARE BLUE
WITH THE FIRST TWO LESS THAN A DYAM APART, AND THE THIRD BEING 40
DYAMS AWAY FROM THEM. THE FIRST STAR HAS A SINGLE PLANET, AND THE
BULK OF THE PLANETS ORBIT THE DISTANT GAMMA COMPANION. THE TENTH
PLANET IS A FELYSIAN WORLD WORTH VISITING FOR THE SPECTACULAR
LIGHTSHOWS CREATED BY AN S08 MULTISTAR SYSTEM.
