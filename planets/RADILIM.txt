Planet: RADILIM
Starmap index: 16
Paris coordinates: 26353616, 6645766, 45295798

Guide entries:
THE THIRD MOON OF THE FIFTH PLANET. ODD COLORATION. FROM SPACE, IT
IS AS THOUGH THE SURFACE GLOWS OF AN ODD GREEN SUBSTANCE. COLORATION
FROM SURFACE RANGES FROM WHITE TO A QUITE BRIGHT GREEN. SOMETIMES
THE SURFACE FEATURES ARE A BIT INTERESTING, WITH THE OCCASIONAL ODD
MOUNTAIN AND SUCH, BUT LITTLE ELSE OF NOTE EXCEPT THE ODD COLOR.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:643:408)
