Guide entries:
COLD, DUSK FELYSIAN PLANET. THE PREDOMINANT FORM OF SURFACE
 LIQUID STAYS IN LIQUID FORM EVEN UNDER -120 DEGREES, BUT THERE
ARE SMALLER LAKES OF SOME OTHER, FROZEN LIQUID AS WELL. THE
GROUND IS FROZEN SOLID, AND I COULD FIND NO PLANT LIFE. ANIMALS,
HOWEVER, ARE RELATIVELY EASY TO FIND. (NOTES BY FARAGO, EPOC6011.
582.716)
AFTER FINDING LIFE ON MYSTERIES, I RETURNED HERE TO EXAMINE THE
PLANET MORE CLOSELY. INDEED, IT DOES SUPPORT PLANT LIFE IN THE
LOW-LYING REGIONS, ONLY THE VEGETATION HERE IS BRIGHT GREEN
RATHER THAN PINK. (NOTE BY FARAGO)
