Guide entries:
THE FOURTH PLANET. A FELYSIAN PLANET. QUITE ODD, THE LANDFORMS ARE
DISTINCTLY VISIBLE. INLAND IS USUALLY INHOSPITABLE FROZEN STRETCHES,
THE REGIONS WITH FLORA TEND TO CONSIST OF ODD AUTUMN COLORS, ORANGE
COLORATIONS ABOUND. LAND FEATURES ARE USUALLY JUST AVERAGE.
