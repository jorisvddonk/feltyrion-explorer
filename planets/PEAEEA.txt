THE FIRST MOON OF THE FOURTH PLANET, A GREEN DUSTY MOON. LAND FEATURES
ARE AVERAGE, WITH SOME CANYONS HERE AND THERE. SOMETIMES, THE COLOR
CAN CAUSE AN UNUSUALLY PRETTY SCENE. COLORS CAN RANGE FROM GREEN TO
DEEP GREEN.
