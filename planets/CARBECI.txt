Planet: CARBECI
Starmap index: 05
Paris coordinates: 26171120, 6391224, 44798266

Guide entries:
THE FIFTH PLANET, A FELYSIAN PLANET. QUITE INTERESTING, ALTHOUGH
INLAND, THERE IS USUALLY A FROZEN, INHOSPITABLE LANDSCAPE, BUT CLOSER
TO THE SHORE, IT BECOMES MORE TEMPERATE, AND PLANT LIFE CAN BE FOUND,
AS WELL AS MANY AVIAN LIFEFORMS. LAND FEATURES ARE AVERAGE. 2 MOONS.
THE SURFACE IS USUALLY GREEN, THE FROZEN AREAS ARE BLUE, AND THE
ATMOSPHERE TENDS TO BE A GREENISH COLOR.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:633:730)
