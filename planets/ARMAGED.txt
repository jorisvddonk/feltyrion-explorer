Planet: ARMAGED
Starmap index: 11
Paris coordinates: 26171120, 6391224, 44798266

Guide entries:
THE SIXTH MOON OF THE FOURTH PLANET, THIS POOR THING HAS BEEN BEAT
TO DEATH, IT HAS MANY LARGE IMPACT CRATERS. AMAZING THAT IT IS STILL
INTACT! THE COLORS RANGE FROM CYAN, PALE GREEN, TO DARKER GREEN WITHIN
THE CRATERS. LAND FEATURES ARE SOMETIMES INTERESTING, SOME CANYONS
AND SUCH HERE AND THERE, NOTHING MUCH ELSE.
