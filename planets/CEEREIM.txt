Planet: CEEREIM

Guide entries:
THE ONLY MOON OF THE FOURTH PLANET, A TAN DUSTY MOON. THE LAND
FEATURES ARE RELATIVELY WORN DOWN, BUT THERE IS STILL THE OCCASIONAL
LARGE CRATER, HILL, MOUNTAIN, OR OTHER SUCH THING. SURFACE COLOR IS
USUALLY A VARYING SHADE OF CREAM OR TAN.
