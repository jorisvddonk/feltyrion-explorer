Planet: PEREPLE
Starmap index: 01
Paris coordinates: 26471120, 6428255, 44500059

Guide entries:
THE ONLY PLANET IN THIS SYSTEM, A QUARTZ WORLD. SURFACE COLOR RANGES
FROM GREY TO PURPLE. LAND FEATURES ARE AVERAGE, WITH OCCASIONAL SPIKES
AND SMALL CANYONS, BUT MOSTLY ROLLING HILLS OF VARYING HEIGHT.
