THE SECOND COMPANION STAR IN THE AMNESIAC SYSTEM.
PABLO HAS 19 PLANETS, THREE OF WHICH ARE FELISIAN WORLDS.
BEING A WHITE DWARF, IT IS UNUSUAL THAT FELISIAN
WORLDS ORBIT THIS STAR.
