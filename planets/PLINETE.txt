Planet: PLINETE
Starmap index: 04
Paris coordinates: 26353616, 6645766, 45295798

Guide entries:
THE FOURTH PLANET. A THIN ATMO PLANET OF LITTLE NOTE, MAINLY
BECAUSE IT IS CONSIDERABLY DARK, EXCEPT AT THE POLES. LAND FEATURES
ARE AVERAGE. COLORS SEEM TO BE YELLOWISH, POLES ARE PINK, WITH A
PURPLE SKY. IT ALSO HAS A SMALL, LOW RING SYSTEM.
