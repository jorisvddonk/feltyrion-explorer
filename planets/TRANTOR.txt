Planet: TRANTOR
Starmap index: 03
Paris coordinates: -1405047568, -17845, 1420680459

Guide entries:
A LUSH, TROPICAL WORLD WITH MORE-THAN-COMFORTABLE TEMPERATURES. ITS
SURFACE WATER IS DIVIDED INTO MICROSCOPIC LAKES, MAKING THE SURFACE
MORE LIKE A MARSH, DEVOID OF LARGE OCEANS OR BODIES OF WATER. EVEN AT
THE POLES, THE TEMPERATURE IS NOT BELOW -20 DEGREES, AND CAN REACH
45 DEGREES NEAR THE EQUATOR. TRANTOR POSESSES A GREEN SKY AND LUSH
GREEN FIELDS. WOULD MAKE A NICE VACATION PLANET.(SCHMERM 6011 598.636)
