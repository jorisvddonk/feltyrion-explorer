Planet: LUMENEP
Starmap index: 02
Paris coordinates: 23736112, 8395872, 49007275

Guide entries:
A GREEN, DUSTY PLANET. THE SECOND PLANET. SURFACE COLOR RANGES FROM
VARIOUS SHADES OF GREEN. SURFACE FEATURES, THOUGH, ARE A LITTLE BELOW
AVERAGE, AND APPEAR TO BE WORN DOWN, PERHAPS OF AGE. ALSO, THERE IS
A SLIGHTLY HIGHER GRAVITY THAN MOST DUSTY PLANETS.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:631:978)
