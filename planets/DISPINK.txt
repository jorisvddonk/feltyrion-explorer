Planet: DISPINK
Starmap index: 25
Paris coordinates: 708048, 4444727, 37468260

Guide entries:
AN AVERAGE THIN ATMO MOON, TOO DARK FOR EXPLORATION IN MANY AREAS,
BUT MUCH BRIGHTER IN THE BRIGHT SPOTS. THE SKY SEEMS TO BE A BLUISH
COLOR, WHILE THE GROUND MAINTAINS A DULL TO HOT PINK COLOR. SURFACE
FEATURES APPEAR TO BE AVERAGE, RANGING FROM SMALL HILLS AND STUFF,
AND THE NORMAL DISSAPEARING FEATURES WITHIN THE BRIGHT SPOTS.
