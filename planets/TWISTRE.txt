Planet: TWISTRE
Starmap index: 13
Paris coordinates: 6212656, 5124804, 40079781

Guide entries:
A PRETTY, FELYSIAN MOON, SIXTH OF THE THIRD PLANET, NAMED SO BECAUSE
THE TREES SOMETIMES ADOPT GNARLED AND SCATTERED BRANCH PATTERNS,
SPREADING EVERYWHERE, SOMETIMES EVEN CAUSING GIANT TREES. THE
ATMOSHPERE IS USUALLY A PURPLISH COLOR, AND THE SURFACE MAINTAINS A
LUSH GREEN IN MOST AREAS. THE PLANET IS A TYPICAL JUNGLE, FEW DESERT
OR UNINHABITED REGIONS.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:631:563)
