Planet: CYPLIST
Starmap index: 10
Paris coordinates: 26708976, 6394413, 44397605

Guide entries:
THE SECOND MOON OF THE SIXTH PLANET, CYAN FROM SPACE, BUT THE SURFACE
IS MORE OF A DULL PURPLE COLOR. SURFACE FEATURES ARE AVERAGE,
SOMETIMES THERE CAN BE PATCHES OF GREY ROCKS HERE AND THERE IN
CERTAIN SECTORS. NOT MUCH ELSE OF NOTE.
