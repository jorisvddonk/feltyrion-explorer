Planet: TIARESS
Starmap index: 04
Paris coordinates: 26453616, 6340673, 45175452

Guide entries:
THE FIRST MOON OF THE THIRD PLANET, A DUSTY MOON THAT IS GREEN FROM
SPACE. LAND COLORATION RANGES FROM YELLOW, TAN, TO EVEN PURPLE.
LAND FEATURES ARE AVERAGE, WITH THE OCCASIONAL UNUSUAL TALL MOUNTAIN,
AND THERE ARE CANYONS, HILLS, CRATERS SOMETIMES, BUT OTHERWISE NOT
MUCH ELSE.
