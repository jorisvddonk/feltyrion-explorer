Planet: YORBAN
Starmap index: 07
Paris coordinates: 3901424, -66380183, -2847415

Guide entries:
THE SEVENTH PLANET OF THIS SYSTEM. YORBAN IS A GREEN GAS GIANT WITH
WHITE CLOUD BANDS, AND IS OVERALL VERY BRIGHT WITH A VERY HIGH ALBEDO.
IT HAS 13 MOONS ORBITING IT, WHICH VARY IN TYPE. THE MOST INTERESTING
TO VISIT IS MOON #7, INNORDICA, WHICH IS A FROZEN FELYSIAN WORLD.
