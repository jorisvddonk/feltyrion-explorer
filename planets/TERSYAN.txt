Planet: TERSYAN
Starmap index: 03
Paris coordinates: 26415760, 6787591, 45079090

Guide entries:
THE THIRD PLANET, A QUITE INTERESTING FELYSIAN PLANET. COLORATION
AT THE EQUATOR TEND TO LACK GRASS, BUT HAVE PLANTS BEARING ORANGE
LEAVES. HOWEVER, HIGHER LATITUDES ARE MORE AUTUMN COLORED. LAND
FEATURES CAN BE QUITE INTERESTING IN SOME SECTORS, SOME RESEMBLING
RUINS. OTHERWISE, A QUITE INTERESTING, ALMOST ENTIRELY HABITABLE
FELYSIAN PLANET, ASIDE FROM WHAT APPEAR TO BE HIGH-ALTITUDE AREAS,
WHERE PLANTS DO NOT SEEM TO GROW, AND THE TEMPERATURE IS MUCH COLDER.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:634:243)
