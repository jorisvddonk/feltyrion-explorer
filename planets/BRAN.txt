Planet: BRAN
Starmap index: 06
Paris coordinates: 5968016, 5031694, 39757796

Guide entries:
AN AVERAGE, DUSTY MOON AROUND THE FELYSIAN PLANET. COLORS ARE USUALLY
SHADES OF A TAN-LIKE COLOR, TO BROWN IN THE DARKER AREAS. LAND FEATURES
ARE AVERAGE, SOMETIMES NON-EXISTANT, MANY OF THE AREAS LANDED IN HAD
ONLY SHARP, LOW HILLS.
