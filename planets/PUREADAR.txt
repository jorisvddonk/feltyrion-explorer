Planet: PUREADAR
Starmap index: 07
Paris coordinates: 26171120, 6391224, 44798266

Guide entries:
SECOND MOON OF FOURTH PLANET, A QUARTZ MOON. A VERY DIM SURFACE, OF
WHICH THE COLOR IS USUALLY A SHADE OF PURPLE, AND THE FEATURES ARE
AVERAGE, BUT MANY BLACK SQUARES COVER THE SURFACE AS SEEN FROM SPACE.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:633:726)
