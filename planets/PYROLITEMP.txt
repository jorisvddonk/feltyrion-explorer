Planet: PYROLITEMP
Starmap index: 03
Paris coordinates: 5968016, 5031694, 39757796

Guide entries:
THE THIRD PLANET IN THIS SYSTEM, A FELYSIAN PLANET. THE NAME IS
MAINLY DERIVED FROM THE FIREY RED TREES NEAR THE EQUATOR AND NEAR
EQUATORIAL REGIONS. ALSO, IT HAS A HIGHER TEMPERATURE THAN THAT
OF MOST FELYSIAN WORLDS. NOT VERY MUCH LAND LIFE SPOTTED ON MOST
OF THE LANDINGS. PLENTY OF AVIAN LIFE. INLAND REGIONS OF THE
PLANET ARE USUALLY DESERTS, EITHER HOT, OR THEY CAN BE COLDER
NEARER THE POLES. LAND FEATURES ARE AVERAGE. MORE LAND THAN WATER.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:631:330)
