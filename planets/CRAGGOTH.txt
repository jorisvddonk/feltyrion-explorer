A CREASED DEADWORLD, 2ND PLANET FROM THE SUN. CRAGGOTH HAS VERY
LITTLE CRATERING, AND A SORT OF GASH/VALLEY AT ONE LOCATION RUNNING
FROM THE EQUATOR TO NEAR THE SOUTHERN POLE. ON THE SURFACE, THE COLOR
OF THE YROUND IS YELLOW, AND THERE WAS AN INTERESTING ROCK FORMATION
TO THE EAST AT THE LANDING SITE 120.71
