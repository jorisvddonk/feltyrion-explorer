THE FIFTH PLANET. A LONELY, PINKISH DUSTY PLANET AS SEEN FROM SPACE,
HOWEVER, THE SURFACE IS USUALLY MORE OF A TAN OR BROWN COLORATION.
NO MOONS. OTHERWISE, IT IS OF LITTLE NOTE.
ALSO HAS A SMALL DEBRIS RING.
