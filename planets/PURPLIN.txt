Planet: PURPLIN
Starmap index: 20
Paris coordinates: 6212656, 5124804, 40079781

Guide entries:
AN AVERAGE THICK ATMO MOON. THIRTEENTH OF THE THIRD PLANET.
SURFACE AREA IS A BEAUTIFUL PURPLE IN MOST AREAS THOUGH, EXCEPT IN
THICKER AREAS, WHERE THE COLOR IS INSTEAD BLUE. PRETTY PLANET, BUT
THE LAND FEATURES ARE AVERAGE. SOMETIMES THE LAND CAN BE FLAT SHADED
COLORS INSTEAD OF THE NORMAL PATTERNED SURFACE.
