Planet: MENIT

Guide entries:
THE FIFTH PLANET, A LONELY ROCKY PLANET. NOT MUCH OF NOTE, ALTHOUGH
THE SURFACE COLOR IS USUALLY VARIOUS SHADES OF TAN OR BROWN. LAND
FEATURES ARE OF LITTLE NOTE. NO MOONS, BUT THERE IS A SMALL DEBRIS RING.
