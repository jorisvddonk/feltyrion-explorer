Planet: MALTAG
Starmap index: 01
Paris coordinates: 3901424, -66380183, -2847415

Guide entries:
MALTAG IS A THINWORLD, WITH A BROWN SURFACE AND YELLOW POLAR CAPS
AS VISIBLE FROM ORBIT. DESPITE ITS PROXIMITY TO THE SUN, THE SURFACE
TEMPERATURE IS ACTUALLY QUITE DECENT, IN THE 30-40 DEGREE RANGE, EVEN
IN DIRECT SUNLIGHT. THE SKY APPEARS BLUE WHEN LANDING IN THE POLAR
REGIONS. (SCHMERM 6011 592.965)
