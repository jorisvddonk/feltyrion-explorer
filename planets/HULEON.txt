Planet: HULEON
Starmap index: 03
Paris coordinates: 21446160, 6389208, 40270646

Guide entries:
THE THRID PLANET, IS FELYSIAN.1 MOON, AND A SMALL RING SYSTEM.
RELATIVELY NICE, BUT AVERAGE. EQUATORIAL REGIONS ARE WARM AND TAKE ON
AUTUMN-ISH COLORS, WHILE HIGHER LATITUDES ARE SLIGHTLY COLDER AND
HAVE MORE GREEN, AS WELL AS PLANTS. LAND FEATURES ARE QUITE LOW AND
AVERAGE.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:633:634)
