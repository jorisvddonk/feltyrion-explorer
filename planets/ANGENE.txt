AN AVERAGE, GREENISH, DUSTY MOON, SEVENTH OF THE THIRD PLANET. LAND
FEATURES ARE AVERAGE, AND APPEAR, IN MOST AREAS, TO BE FAIRLY WORN DOWN
FROM AGE, AS THERE ARE FEW SHARP LAND FEATURES IN MOST OF THE LANDING
AREAS. COLORS VARY FROM GREEN, TO YELLOW, AND PERHAPS OTHER COLORS.
