Planet: AUTGEL
Starmap index: 04
Paris coordinates: 26646832, 6023437, 44755504

Guide entries:
THE FOURTH PLANET, A BEAUTIFUL FELYSIAN PLANET. THE MAJORITY OF THE
PLANET IS COVERED WITH AUTUMN COLORED FOREST OR GRASSLANDS, WITH
OCCASIONAL SPOTS OF DEEPER GREEN JUNGLE. MUCH FAUNA AS WELL, ESPECIALLY
LARGE HOPPERS. LAND FEATURES ARE AVERAGE. 3 MOONS.
