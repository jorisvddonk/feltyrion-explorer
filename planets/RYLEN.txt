Planet: RYLEN
Starmap index: 02
Paris coordinates: -1997899152, 41706, 1210090

Guide entries:
THE SECOND PLANET, A LIGHT BLUE THICK ATMO WORLD, WITH A SMALL DEBRIS
RING. VERY PRETTY. SURFACE AREA COLORATION USUALLY RANGES FROM ORANGE
TO TAN, AS WELL AS SHADES AROUND THERE. LAND FEATURES GREATLY VERY,
BUT LARGE CALDERAS SEEM TO BE QUITE COMMON. CANYONS ARE HERE AND THERE
AS WELL, AS WELL AS HILLS AND SUCH. QUITE A PRETTY PLANET.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:648:189)
