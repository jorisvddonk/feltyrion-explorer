Planet: PAREOYSIAN
Starmap index: 12
Paris coordinates: 22364336, 7906996, 48257710

Guide entries:
A FELYSIAN MOON, THE SECOND OF THE FOURTH PLANET. VERY BRIGHT.
THE GRASS TENDS TO BE A VERY BRIGHT NEON GREEN, WHILE OTHER FLORA
GROW TO HAVE PURPLE LEAVES. ALMOST THE ENTIRE PLANET IS HABITABLE
,ALTHOUGH COLDER THAN USUAL. LAND FEATURES ARE ALSO ABOVE AVERAGE,
RANGING FROM ODD FEATURES, LIKE CANYONS, ODD PITS, AND ODD
MOUNTAINS. THE ATMOSPHERE IS USUALLY A BLUISH COLOR, BUT CAN
SOMETIMES BE PURPLISH. THE SUNSETS ARE QUITE BEAUTIFUL AS WELL.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:631:910)
