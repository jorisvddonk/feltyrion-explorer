Planet: HONEYDROP
Starmap index: 37
Paris coordinates: 708048, 4444727, 37468260

Guide entries:
A BEAUTIFUL, ALTHOUGH SLIGHTLY DARK, FELYSIAN MOON. ALMOST ALL LAND
AREAS ARE HABITABLE, THERE BEING ALMOST NO DESERT AT ALL. NAMED SO
BECAUSE SOMETIMES THE GRASS AND EVEN SOMETIMES THE TREES ADOPT A
BEAUTIFUL HONEYLIKE COLOR. LAND FEATURES ARE AVERAGE, NOTHING MUCH
OF REAL INTEREST. ALSO THERE SEEMS TO BE NOT VERY MUCH LAND LIFE.
PLENTY OF BIRDS, HOWEVER.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:630:269)
