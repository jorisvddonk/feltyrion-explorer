Guide entries:
PRETTY INTERESTING MOON, SURFACE COLOR RANGES FROM YELLOW TO GREENISH
SHADES, SURFACE FEATURES ARE NOT MUCH, NOT VERY MANY MINOR CRATERS, BUT
MANY TALL, SMOOTH HILLS AND LOW VALLEYS, MAKING IT INTERESTING TO
EXPLORE. THE POOR THING ALSO LOOKS LIKE IT GOT REALLY BEAT UP IN ITS
PAST, SINCE THERE ARE MANY HUGE CRATERS ON IT.
