Planet: NOONERAAN
Starmap index: 10
Paris coordinates: 3901424, -66380183, -2847415

Guide entries:
FARTHEST PLANET IN THIS SYSTEM, WITH AN ORBITAL PERIOD OF ALMOST 4
EPOCS. NOONERAAN IS A QUARTZWORLD WITH AN OXYGEN ATMOSPHERE, HAVING
A PRESSURE ALMOST EQUAL TO 1. THE TEMPERATURE IS OBVIOUSLY VERY
COLD, HOWEVER. THE PLANET APPEARS GREEN FROM ORBIT, BUT THE SURFACE
IS ACTUALLY BROWN. A SINGLE MOON ORBITS THIS PLANET.
