Planet: PENTER
Starmap index: 09
Paris coordinates: 23736112, 8395872, 49007275

Guide entries:
A DUSTY MOON, THE THIRD OF THE THIRD PLANET. SURFACE FROM SPACE IS
DOTTED WITH CONSIDERABLY LARGE CRATERS. SURFACE COLOR IS USUALLY A
PINKISH COLOR. SURFACE FEATURES ARE AVERAGE, A LITTLE WORN DOWN, BUT
THERE SEEMS TO BE QUITE A FEW CANYONS THAT CAN BE SEEN OFF IN THE
DISTANCE.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:632:425)
