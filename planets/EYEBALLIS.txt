PLANET P03 DISCOVERED BY MCWGOGS EPOC 6011.639.664. CRATEIZED .
PLANET HAS A HUGE DARK CRATER.LANDSCAPE IN THE CRATER IS DARK BLUE,
BUT MINERALS SHINE FROM DISTANCE. I'VE SEEN STRANGE PUDDLES OF
BRIGHTER ROCK. I'VE FOUND NO BIG ROCKS IN THE CRATER, THOUGH AREA
OUTSIDE THE CRATER IS FULL OF THEM. VIEW FROM ORBIT SHOWS THAT THE
MINERALS WERE THROWED OUT FROM THE CRATER FORMING BRIGHT RAYS.
THERE ARE NO BIG ROCKS IN THE RAYS NEITHER. IT SEEMS THAT THERE WERE
CRUSHED TO DUST BY PRESSURE AND TEMP. AT THE MOMENT OF IMPACT.
NOTES BY MCWGOGS EPOC 6011.639.665
