Planet: DLIEREK
Starmap index: 01
Paris coordinates: 26240048, 6346979, 44803770

Guide entries:
THE FIRST PLANET, A DIM QUARTZ WORLD. BLUE-PURPLE FROM SPACE, BUT OF
COURSE, MUCH DARKER ON THE SURFACE. NOT MUCH OF NOTE, ALTHOUGH THERE
ARE USUALLY ROLLING HILLS WITH THE OCCASIONAL SHARP POINT HERE AND
THERE. THE SURFACE IS USUALLY TOO DARK TO SEE.
