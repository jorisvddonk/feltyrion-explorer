Planet: PIRUNING
Starmap index: 04
Paris coordinates: -1290448, 2491256, 22212119

Guide entries:
VERY DARK PLANET, ONLY REAL EXPLORABLE REGIONS BEING BRIGHT SPOTS AND
POLES, WHERE IT CAN JUST BARELY BE MADE OUT OF A RED SURFACE, AND
BLUE ATMOSHPERE. THE PLANET IS COVERED WITH HILLS AND SUCH, NOT MUCH
ELSE. IT ALSO HAS A RING SYSTEM, AND ONE MOON.
IT IS POSSIBLE THAT IT AND ITS MOON ARE IN A BINARY ORBIT, AS THEY
AS THEY ARE ABOUT THE SAME SIZE.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:624:127)
