Planet: PINKEK
Starmap index: 29
Paris coordinates: 708048, 4444727, 37468260

Guide entries:
AN AVERAGE, PINK, DUSTY MOON. SURFACE COLORS ARE ALMOST ALL OF PINK
SHADES, AND THE LAND FEATURES ARE AVERAGE, BUT SOMETIMES INTERESTING
FEATURES SUCH AS ABNORMALLY TALL MOUNTAINS, AND OTHER SUCH STRUCTURES,
CAN BE FOUND.
