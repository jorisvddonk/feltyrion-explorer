Planet: JUNGAL
Starmap index: 03
Paris coordinates: -1290448, 2491256, 22212119

Guide entries:
PLANET IS FULL OF LIFE, WATER, HILLS, ISLANDS, AND OTHER SUCH THINGS
POPULATE THE PLANET. FORESTED AREAS ARE VERY COMMON, AND ALSO SOME
GRASSLANDS. MOST NOTABLE ARE THE GIANT HOPPERS THAT POPULATE THE LAND,
ESPECIALLY IN CERTAIN AREAS. OTHER LIFEFORMS ARE PRESENT, TOO.
THE PLANET ALSO HAS 3 MOONS.
