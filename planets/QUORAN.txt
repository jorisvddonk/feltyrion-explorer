Planet: QUORAN
Starmap index: 06
Paris coordinates: -2233584, 33860885, 5055785

Guide entries:
 THE SECOND FELISIAN WORLD IN THE HOME SWEET PIXEL SYSTEM.
TOUGH BEING MUCH COLDER AND MUCH FARTHER AWAY THEN ALONA, THIS WORLD IS
ALSO IMPRESSIVLY BEAUTIFUL - TOUGH MOSTLY BEING COLD DESSERTS AND
FROZEN OCEANS.
SUPRISING TO FIND A WORLD THIS FAR OUT, BUT NOT UNHEARD OF.
QUORAN ALSO REFLECTS SUNLIGHT IN A WAY WHICH MAKES IT SPARKLE LIKE A
BLUE PERL. VERY GOOD PHOTO-OPERTUNITY.
(STARGAZER. EPOC 6011:612:391)
