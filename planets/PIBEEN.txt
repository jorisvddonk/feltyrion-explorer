Planet: PIBEEN
Starmap index: 05
Paris coordinates: 26553616, 6466652, 45080159

Guide entries:
THE ONLY MOON OF THE THIRD PLANET, A PINK DUSTY MOON. THE LAND FEATURES
ARE WORN DOWN, WITH LITTLE OF INTEREST EXCEPT LOW, HILLS AND SUCH.
THE COLORS ARE PRETTY, THOUGH, RANGING FROM PINK, YELLOW, TO A DARK
PURPLE-ISH COLOR.
