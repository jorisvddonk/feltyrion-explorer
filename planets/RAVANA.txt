Guide entries:
A SMALL GREEN AND TEMPERATE FELYSIAN WORLD, AND CLOSEST INHABITABLE
WORLD TO THE FARHOME SYSTEM. THE ATMOSPHERIC PRESSURE IS PERFECT, BEING
BETWEEN 1.002 AND 1.007 BUT THE GRAVITY IS ABOUT 0.550.
AT 125.40 THERE ARE TWO SMALL ISLANDS, ONE OF WHICH HAS A RIVER LEADING
INTO A HUGE CRAGGY MOUNTAIN.
AT 333.87 THERE IS A LARGE GREEN FOREST OF TREES, SOME OF WHICH ARE
TRANSPARENT, AND A SMALL ISLAND OFF THE COAST.
AT 356.111 NEAR THE SOUTH POLE, THERE IS AN IMPACT CRATER.
THERE ARE DISTINCT LARGE LAND MASSES AND CONTINENTS, AND THE SURFACE
IS ABOUT 40-50% WATER OR ICE. (SCHMERM 6011 593.795)
