Planet: IMPACT
Starmap index: 06

A ROCKY PLANET, THE SIXTH PLANET. NO MOONS. COLOR FROM SPACE IS GREY,
BUT ON THE SURFACE, ITS MORE OF A PALE PINK WITH A LITTLE PURPLE MIXED
IN. SURFACE FEATURES ARE AVERAGE. MANY MASSIVE IMPACT CRATERS DOT THE
PLANET.
