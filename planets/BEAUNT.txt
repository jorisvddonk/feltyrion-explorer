Planet: BEAUNT
Starmap index: 02
Paris coordinates: 22364336, 7906996, 48257710

Guide entries:
A BLUE, THIN ATMO PLANET, THE SECOND PLANET. SURFACE IS BLUE,
ATMOSHPERE IS ORANGE. THE BRIGHT AREAS OF THE PLANET ARE QUITE BRIGHT,
ACTUALLY. THE TERRAIN, THOUGH, IS VERY FEATURELESS AND FLAT IN MOST
AREAS. NOTHING MUCH SPOTTED BUT VERY, VERY SMALL HILLS, AND ROCKS.
ALSO HAS A SMALL RING SYSTEM.
