Planet: FELIS 1
Starmap index: 01
Paris coordinates: -1626800, 33322066, 4959862

Guide entries:
 THE FIRST PLANET IN THE FELIS SYSTEM, APPEARS TO BE A SMALL,
MARS-LIKE WORLD, TOUGH WITH SIGNIFICANTLY HIGHER TEMPERATURES THEN
MARS.
BUT BEING THE FIRST PLANET, THIS WORLD IS REALLY NOT THAT HOT,
TEMPERATURES REACHING UP TO 64 DEGREES CENTIGRADE AT THE EQUATORIAL
AREAS.
NOTHING OF REAL INTEREST WAS FOUND UPON LANDING, TOUGH AT EACH LANDING
I HAPPENED TO FALL INTO ONE OF THE SEVERAL HUGE CHASMS ON THIS PLANETS
SURFACE.
THE PLANET ALSO SPORTS A SET OF NICE RINGS.
(STARGAZER. EPOC 6011:611:180)
