Planet: DELERNG
Starmap index: 06
Paris coordinates: 26171120, 6391224, 44798266

Guide entries:
THE FIRST MOON OF THE FOURTH PLANET, A THIN ATMO. THE SURFACE, EXCEPT
AT THE POLES, IS VERY DARK, BUT A BLUE ATMOSPHERE AND AN ORANGE-RED
SURFACE CAN BE SEEN. SOMETIMES THE FEATURES ON THE SURFACE TEND TO BE
ABOVE AVERAGE, BUT LITTLE ELSE OF NOTE.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:633:726)
