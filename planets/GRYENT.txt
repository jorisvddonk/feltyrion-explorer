THE SECOND MOON OF THE THIRD PLANET, A DUSTY MOON THAT IS BRIGHT BLUE
FROM SPACE. LAND FEATURES ARE AVERAGE WITH THE OCCASIONAL CANYON,
HILL, AND TALL CRATER. SURFACE COLORATION RANGES FROM GREY, LIGHT BLUE,
AND SOME OTHER MIXES.
