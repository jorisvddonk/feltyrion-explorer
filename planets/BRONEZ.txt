Planet: BRONEZ
Starmap index: 04
Paris coordinates: 26553616, 6466652, 45080159

Guide entries:
THE FOURTH PLANET, A DUSTY PLANET THAT APPEARS OF BRONZE COLORATION
FROM SPACE. LAND FEATURES ARE RELATIVELY WORN DOWN, WITH THE OCCASIONAL
CRATER, MOUNTAIN, AND HILLS, BUT OTHERWISE THERE ISN'T MUCH ELSE OF
NOTE. HAS ONE MOON, CEEREIM.
