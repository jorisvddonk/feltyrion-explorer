Planet: KHASYEN
Starmap index: 18
Paris coordinates: 23736112, 8395872, 49007275

Guide entries:
A DESERT FELYSIAN MOON, THE FIRST OF THE FOURTH PLANET. THE PLANET HAS
FEW HABITABLE ZONES, HOWEVER, THIS DOES NOT PREVENT IT FROM HAVING
FAUNA. IT IS A MODERATE TEMPERATURE AS WELL. COLORS ARE USUALLY
GREENISH, YELLOWISH, AND OTHER WEIRD COLORS, AND THE SKY IS USUALLY
AN ODD, VERY PALE TAN-GREEN COLOR. SURFACE FEATURES ARE AVERAGE.
FLORA USUALLY HAVE ORANGE LEAVES.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:632:432)
