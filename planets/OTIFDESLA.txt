Planet: OTIFDESLA
Starmap index: 07
Paris coordinates: 5009808, 4830465, 39078705

Guide entries:
A PRETTY FELYSIAN PLANET AROUND THE SECOND STAR. THE FLORA LEAF
COLOR RANGES FROM GREEN TO A VERY PRETTY PURPLE COLOR. NOT MUCH
FAUNA THOUGH, SO FAR I'VE ONLY SEEN SMALL BLUE HOPPERS AND BIRDS.
AVERAGE TERRAIN, BUT PRETTY NEVERTHELESS. ALSO PRETTY COLD IN MOST
AREAS, BUT THE FLORA THRIVES ON THE MAJORITY OF THE PLANET.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:631:316)
