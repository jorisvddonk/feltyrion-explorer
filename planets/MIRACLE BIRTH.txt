Planet: MIRACLE BIRTH
Starmap index: 32
Paris coordinates: 5438192, 26445464, -185384

Guide entries:
AN EXTREMELY RARE FIND, A FELISIAN PLANET (MOON, ACTUALLY) IN A
S09 SYSTEM. ANIMAL LIFE IS PLENTIFUL IN THE COLD HIGHER REGIONS
AS WELL AS IN THE LOWLANDS. PLANTS ARE HARDER TO FIND, BUT I
HAVE FOUND AN ISLAND DENSELY OVERGROWN WITH TREES AND BUSHES,
AND IT IS VERY LIKELY THAT THERE ARE OTHER SIMILAR LOCATIONS AS
WELL. (NOTES BY FARAGO, 6011.582.860)
ALSO, THE MOON IS IN CLOSE ORBIT TO ITS PLANET. WHENEVER IT
PASSES IN FRONT OF IT, YOU CAN SEE THAT ITS ATMOSPHERE IS
EXTREMELY THICK. (NOTE BY FARAGO)
