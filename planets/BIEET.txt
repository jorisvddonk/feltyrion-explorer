Planet: BIEET
Starmap index: 03
Paris coordinates: 26553616, 6466652, 45080159

Guide entries:
THE THIRD PLANET, A TAN THICK ATMO PLANET. THE SURFACE AREA COLOR RANGES
FROM DIFFERENT SHADES OF DARK BLUE. LAND FEATURES ARE AVERAGE, ALTHOUGH
IT IS A BIT HARD TO SEE THEM, EVEN IN THE AREAS WITH LOWER DENSITY.
IT HAS A LARGE, PRETTY RING SYSTEM AT AN ODD ANGLE IN RELATION TO THE
PLANET, AND IT HAS ONE MOON, CALLED PIBEEN.
