Planet: TEALIN
Starmap index: 06
Paris coordinates: -1290448, 2491256, 22212119

Guide entries:
COLORS RANGE FROM A NICE TEAL COLOR TO A FEW OTHER SHADES, MOSTLY TEAL
THOUGH. MOON MAY BE CONSIDERABLY ANCIENT, AS THE MOON LACKS SHARP
FEATURES. NICE TO EXPLORE, THOUGH. FEW CRATERS, AND LAND FEATURES ARE
VERY SMOOTH, MOSTLY HILLS AND SMALL VALLEYS.
