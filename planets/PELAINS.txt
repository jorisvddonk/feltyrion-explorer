Planet: PELAINS
Starmap index: 03
Paris coordinates: 26346832, 6741064, 45112797

Guide entries:
THE THIRD PLANET, A RATHER PRETTY, AND SOMEWHAT UNUSUAL, FELYSIAN
PLANET, WITH ONE MOON. MOST BRIGHT SPOTS, WHICH COVER MOST OF THE
PLANET, MAY BE HIGH-ALTITUDE AREAS, WHERE THERE IS NO PLANT LIFE AND A
SOMEWHAT COLDER TEMPERATURE. HOWEVER, DARK SPOTS ARE MUCH MORE
HABITABLE, WHERE, IN THE EQUATORIAL REGIONS AND N. HEMISPHERE, THE
COLORS ARE MAINLY OLIVE-ISH, WHILE IN THE S. HEMISPHERE, SOMETIMES
THEY MAY BE CHOCOLATE COLORED. SURFACE FEATURES TEND TO BE AVERAGE.
FEW LARGE BODIES OF WATER ARE VISIBLE FROM SPACE.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:634:165)
