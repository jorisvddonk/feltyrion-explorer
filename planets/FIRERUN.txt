Planet: FIRERUN
Starmap index: 03
Paris coordinates: 708048, 4444727, 37468260

Guide entries:
AN INTERESTING FELYSIAN PLANET. RATHER UNUSUAL, BECAUSE THE MAJORITY
OF THE PLANET IS COVERED IN AN EXPANSIVE DESERT, AREAS WITH LIFE
USUALLY ONLY BY THE COASTLINES OF THE SMALL BODIES OF WATER. SURFACE
COLORS RANGE FROM TAN, OLIVE, AND FIREY ORANGE, HENCE THE NAME.
ALSO, SOMETIMES INTERESTING FEATURES CAN BE FOUND, WHAT LOOK LIKE
ANCIENT RUINS, LIKE CASTLES, TALL BUILDINGS, AND OTHER SUCH FORMS.
PLANET HAS ONE MOON AS WELL.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:630:86)
