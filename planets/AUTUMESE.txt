Planet: AUTUMESE
Starmap index: 25
Paris coordinates: 6212656, 5124804, 40079781

Guide entries:
 A FELYSIAN MOON, THE EIGHTEENTH OF THE THIRD PLANET, NAMED SO
BECAUSE AREAS WITH FLORA TEND TO HAVE ORANGE COLORS, ESPECIALLY
THE GRASS. INTERIORS OF THE CONTINENTS, HOWEVER, USUALLY TEND TO BE
A DESERT. OTHERWISE, THE MOON IS AVERAGE. THE ATMOSHPERE TENDS TO BE
YELLOWISH, WITH A HINT OF GREEN IN AREAS WITH FLORA.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:631:571)
