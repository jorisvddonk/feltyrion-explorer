Planet: BREYUS
Starmap index: 03
Paris coordinates: 3901424, -66380183, -2847415

Guide entries:
BREYUS, THE THIRD PLANET IN THIS SYSTEM, SHOULD BE ITS OWN PLANETARY
SYSTEM CONSIDERING IT HAS TWENTY MOONS. TWO OF THOSE MOONS, #3 AND #9,
ARE FELYSIAN. THE DISTANCES BETWEEN MOONS IN THIS MEGAPLANET'S
DOMAIN CAN BE OVER 1 DYAM. BREYUS IS A BRIGHT YELLOW, ALMOST SUN-LIKE
AND GLOWING LIKE A S00 STAR, AND HAS A LARGE AND WIDE RING SYSTEM
AROUND IT. (SCHMERM 6011 593.48)
