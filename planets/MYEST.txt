Planet: MYEST
Starmap index: 05
Paris coordinates: 26353616, 6645766, 45295798

Guide entries:
THE FIFTH PLANET. A PRETTY FELYSIAN PLANET, WAY BACK HERE IN THE LAST
SLOT OF THE SYSTEM. SURPRISINGLY LIVELY AND WARM. THE EQUATORIAL REGIONS
ARE A RELATIVELY TEMPERATE DESERT, HIGHER LATITUDES TEND TO HAVE MORE
FAUNA AND FLORA AROUND. 3 MOONS. WIDE VARIETY OF COLORS, BUT THE SKY
TENDS TO BE TAN OR GREY IN COLORATION, BUT THE LANDSCAPE VARIES.
DESERT REGIONS TEND TO BE DULL PINK OR RED THOUGH.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:643:407)
