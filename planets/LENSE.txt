Guide entries:
THE THIRD PLANET, A DUSTY PLANET. ONE MOON. QUITE INTERESTING.
COLORS RANGE FROM PINK TO OLIVE, BUT MOST OF ALL, ITS SURFACE IS CLEAR.
PLUS, THE LAND FEATURES CAN BE QUITE INTERESTING, WITH LARGE CRATERS,
HILLS, AND SUCH.
