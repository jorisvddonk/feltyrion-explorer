THE FIRST PLANET IN THIS SYSTEM, A PURPLE QUARTZ WORLD. SURFACE COLOR
RANGES FROM PALE PURPLE, GREY, TO GREEN IN THE BRIGHTER AREAS. QUITE
PRETTY. LAND FEATURES INCLUDE LARGE HILLS, AND THE OCCASIONAL SHARP,
SMALL HILL. ALSO, THERE ARE VERY MANY CLEAR ROCKS.
