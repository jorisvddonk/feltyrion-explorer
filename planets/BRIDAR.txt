Planet: BRIDAR
Starmap index: 08
Paris coordinates: 22364336, 7906996, 48257710

Guide entries:
A STRANGE CONTRAST OF DARK AND LIGHT ON THIS QUARTZ PLANET, THE EIGHTH
PLANET. IT HAS 3 MOONS. THE ATMOSHPERE IS VERY BRIGHT, AND THE LAND IS
CONSIDERABLY DARK, AND AVERAGE.
THIS PLANET ALSO HAS A COOL RING SYSTEM, AT A NEAR VERTICAL
ARRANGEMENT TO THE SURFACE.
