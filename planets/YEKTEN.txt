Planet: YEKTEN
Starmap index: 02
Paris coordinates: -1996709872, 7885, 1308189

Guide entries:
THE SECOND PLANET, A ROCKY PLANET. NO MOONS. COLORATION IS THE SAME AS      
IT'S NEARBY COUSIN, WITH VARIATIONS OF TAN, ORANGE, AND BROWN. LAND         
FEATURES ARE USUALLY AVERAGE, WITH FLAT-TOPPED HILLS AND SOME SECTORS       
LACKING THEM ALTOGETHER, AS WELL AS OCCASIONAL BUMPS IN THE GROUND.         
THERE IS STILL THE POSSIBILITY FOR AN ODD CANYON OR A MOUNTAIN OF           
SORTS.
