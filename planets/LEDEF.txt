Planet: LEDEF
Starmap index: 01
Paris coordinates: 26471120, 6328255, 44558202

Guide entries:
THE FIRST PLANET IN THIS SYSTEM, A RED ROCKY PLANET OF NOT MUCH NOTE.
LAND FEATURES ARE AVERAGE, WITH THE OCCASIONAL FLAT TOPPED HILLS, BUT
OTHERWISE IT IS RELATIVELY FLAT. COLOR RANGES FROM DIM RED, DIM PINK,
TO DIM TAN.
