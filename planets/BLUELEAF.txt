A FELYSIAN MOON, THE THIRD OF THE FOURTH PLANET. AN AVERAGE JUNGLE
PLANET, QUITE PRETTY, AND COVERED WITH PLANTS, OTHERWISE, A TYPICAL

BLUE COLOR, WHILE THE SURFACE MAINTAINS A GREEN COLOR.
