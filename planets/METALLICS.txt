A VERY BEAUTIFUL AND INTERESTING PLANET, NOT FOR THE LAND FEATURES,
BUT ITS COLORS. THE COLORS RANGE FROM GOLD, BRONZE, TO EVEN SILVER,
WHICH LEADS ME TO BELIEVE THAT THIS PLANET MANY IN FACT CONSIST
OF THESE VERY METALS! OTHERWISE, THE LAND FEATURES ARENT TOO
SPECTACULAR, A CRATER HERE AND THERE, AND SOME VERY LOW HILLS.
