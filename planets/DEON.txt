Guide entries:
THE FIRST MOON OF THE FOURTH PLANET, A DUSTY MOON. COLORATION RANGES
FROM GREEN TO YELLOW, AND SOME AREAS, GREY. LAND FEATURES ARE RELATIVELY
AVERAGE, SOMETIMES ABOVE AVERAGE, LIKE STRANGE MOUNTAINS AND SUCH.
OTHERWISE, QUITE AVERAGE.
