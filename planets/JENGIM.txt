Planet: JENGIM
Starmap index: 27
Paris coordinates: 6212656, 5124804, 40079781

Guide entries:
A DIM MOON, THE SECOND OF THE FOURTH PLANET. FELYSIAN. WHAT IS
INTERESTING IS THAT THE PLANET HAS VERY FEW TO NO LARGE BODIES OF
WATER, BUT STILL IS QUITE HABITABLE AND HAS ABUNDANT FLORAL LIFE.
THE ATMOSHPERE IS A SLIGHTLY DIM PURPLE COLOR. MOST OF THE PLANET IS
QUITE HABITABLE, ALTHOUGH DIM.LAND FEATURES ARE AVERAGE, SOMETIMES
PONDS AND LAKES AND SUCH CAN BE FOUND THOUGH.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:631:572)
