YELOOW-PURPLE BODIES OF WATER. I HAVE ALSO FOUND CAT-LIKE YELLOW

I SUSPECT THESE CREATURES ARE VERY INTELLIGENT.I DIDN'T FIND ANY OF
THOSE ON OTHER PLANETS YET.
