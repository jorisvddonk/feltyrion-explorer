Planet: JUNGELGE
Starmap index: 04
Paris coordinates: 26546832, 6418955, 44905877

Guide entries:
THE FOURTH PLANET, A RATHER NICE, ALMOST ALL AROUND HABITABLE JUNGLE
FELYSIAN PLANET. LAND FEATURES TEND TO BE AVERAGE, SOMETIMES
INTERESTING. LEAVES TEND TO BE SORTA ORANGE, AND HABITABLE REGIONS
TEND TO BE QUITE GREEN ASIDE FROM LEAVES. ALSO, IT HAS A SMALL
RING SYSTEM.
