Planet: BIREETS
Starmap index: 08

Guide entries:

PLANET. SURFACE FEATURES ARE AVERAGE, WITH OCCASIONAL SPOTS OF LOW
JAGGED HILLS. SURFACE COLOR USUALLY IS YELLOWISH, WITH A GREENISH SKY.
