Planet: DISMIC
Starmap index: 16
Paris coordinates: 6212656, 5124804, 40079781

Guide entries:
A POOR FELYSIAN PLANET, SEEMS TO BE FROZEN OVER. REGIONS WITH FLORA
ARE VERY RARE, AND LARGE BODIES OF WATER ARE NEARLY NON-EXISTANT.
THE FAUNA THRIVES, THOUGH. ATMOSHPERIC COLOR IS SOMEWHAT YELLOWISH,
GREEN IN REGIONS WITH FLORA. SOMETIMES WEIRD MOUNTAINS CAN BE FOUND AS
WELL, I LIKE TO THINK THEY MAY BE ANCIENT RUINS, THOUGH.
NINTH MOON OF THE THIRD PLANET.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:631:565)
