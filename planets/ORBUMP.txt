Planet: ORBUMP
Starmap index: 20
Paris coordinates: 708048, 4444727, 37468260

Guide entries:
AN AVERAGE ROCKY PLANET, ITS COLORS RANGE FROM ORANGE, STRAW, GRAY, AND
OTHER SUCH COLORS. THE LAND FEATURES ARE AVERAGE, BUT SOMETIMES
SOMEWHAT UNUSUAL FEATURES CAN BE FOUND, SUCH AS CANYONS AND ODD
CRATERLIKE THINGS.
