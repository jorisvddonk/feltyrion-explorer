Planet: TEYSAN
Starmap index: 03
Paris coordinates: 26171120, 6391224, 44798266

Guide entries:
THE THIRD PLANET, A FELYSIAN PLANET. A QUITE NICE, AVERAGE, HABITABLE
PLANET WITH FEW INHOSPITABLE AREAS, HOWEVER, NOT VERY MANY LARGE
TREES EITHER. MANY HILLS, HOWEVER. LAND FEATURES TEND TO BE AVERAGE.
THE GRASS IS ALMOST ALWAYS GREEN, AND THE LEAVES ARE A BROWN COLOR.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:633:725)
