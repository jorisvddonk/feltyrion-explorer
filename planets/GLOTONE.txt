Planet: GLOTONE
Starmap index: 06
Paris coordinates: 26646832, 6023437, 44755504

Guide entries:
THE SECOND MOON OF THE FOURTH PLANET, A DUSTY MOON. LAND FEATURES ARE
RELATIVELY SMOOTH ON THIS PRETTY BLUE PLANET. COLORS RANGE FROM A
LIGHT BLUE TO A VERY PALE PURPLE. THE MOON IS NAMED SO BECAUSE THE
SURFACE IS MAINLY THE SAME GLOWING COLOR ALL AROUND.
