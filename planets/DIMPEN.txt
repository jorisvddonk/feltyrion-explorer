Planet: DIMPEN
Starmap index: 08
Paris coordinates: 23736112, 8395872, 49007275

Guide entries:
A LUMPY, DUSTY MOON, THE SECOND OF THE THIRD PLANET. ALTHOUGH FROM
SPACE, IT APPEARS BROWNISH, THE SURFACE IS USUALLY A GREYISH OR
GREENISH SHADE. SURFACE FEATURES ARE AVERAGE, SOMETIMES INTERESTING.
