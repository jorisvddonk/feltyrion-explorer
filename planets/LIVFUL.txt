Planet: LIVFUL
Starmap index: 05
Paris coordinates: 21446160, 6389208, 40270646

Guide entries:
A PRETTY FELYSIAN PLANET, THE FIFTH PLANET. 2 MOONS. PRETTY MUCH IS A
JUNGLE, SOMETIMES COLD, BUT COVERED WITH PLANTS, NEARLY EVERYWHERE.
LAND FEATURES CAN BE SOMETIMES BIZARRE TOO, WITH STRANGE FEATURES
JUTTING OUT OF THE LANDSCAPE IN CERTAIN SECTORS, SOMETIMES RESEMBLING
A TALL, ANCIENT, GRASS COVERED RUIN OR SOMETHING. COLORS ARE NORMAL,
GREEN LANDSCAPE, BUT A YELLOWISH ATMOSPHERE. LEAVES TEND TO BE GREEN.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:633:637)
