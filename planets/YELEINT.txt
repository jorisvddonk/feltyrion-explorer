Planet: YELEINT
Starmap index: 33
Paris coordinates: 6212656, 5124804, 40079781

Guide entries:
A SOMEWHAT INTERESTING DUSTY MOON AROUND THE FIFTH PLANET. THE ONLY
MOON. THE SURFACE COLORS RANGE FROM GREENISH-YELLOW, TO A WEIRD YELLOW
COLOR. SURFACE FEATURES ARE SOMETIMES INTERESTING, WITH ODD CANYONS
AND MOUNTAINS AND SUCH.
