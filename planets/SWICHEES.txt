Planet: SWICHEES
Starmap index: 18
Paris coordinates: 708048, 4444727, 37468260

Guide entries:
AN ODD PLANET, INSTEAD OF ITS CRATERS BEING DARKER THAN THE REST
OF THE PLANET, THEY ARE ALMOST PURE WHITE! SURFACE COLORS RANGE FROM
A SORTA SWISS CHEESE COLOR, HENCE THE NAME, TO WHITE, AND SOMETIMES
EVEN A GREYISH COLOR. LAND FEATURES ARE SOMETIMES ODD, SOMETIMES
STRANGE PITS, CRATERS, OR CANYONS CAN BE FOUND.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:630:169)
