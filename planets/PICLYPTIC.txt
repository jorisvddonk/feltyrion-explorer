THE FIFTH PLANET. THIN ATMO, ONE MOON. COLORS RANGE FROM A DEEP
RED SKY AND DARK BLUE SURFACE, TO A BEAUTIFUL BRIGHT PINK SKY AND
PURPLE SURFACE AT THE POLES. LAND FEATURES ARE AVERAGE, AND
THE BRIGHTNESS IS AVERAGE.
