Planet: NOMMOS MINOR
Starmap index: 26
Paris coordinates: 3625712, -66300682, -2983002

Guide entries:
A DARK DULL GREEN GAS GIANT MOON, WHICH IS SEVERELY BELOW THE
ORBITAL PLANE OF THE OTHER MOONS IN THIS GAS GIANT SYSTEM.
IT IS DARK DULL GREEN WITH WISPY BANDS OF BRIGHT GREEN AND BRIGHT
REFLECTIVE SPOTS. THE ORBITAL ANGLE AND THE FACT THAT THIS IS
THE LAST MOON IN THE SYSTEM SUGGESTS THAT NOMMOS MINOR WAS
AN INDEPENDENT PLANET CAPTURED A LONG TIME AGO.
(SCHMERM 6011 594.63)
