A CYAN, DUSTY MOON, THE ONLY ONE OF THE FIFTH PLANET. COLORS ARE CYAN TO
GREYISH-CYAN. LAND FEATURES ARE SMOOTH, BUT AVERAGE. THE SMOOTHNESS MAY
INDICATE THE AGE OF THIS MOON IS A LITTLE HIGH.
