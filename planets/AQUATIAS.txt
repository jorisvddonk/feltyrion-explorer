Planet: AQUATIAS
Starmap index: 16
Paris coordinates: 708048, 4444727, 37468260

Guide entries:
A RATHER UNUSUAL FELYSIAN PLANET, THE MAJORITY OF THE PLANET IS
COVERED IN WATER, OR LOW LYING LANDS SURROUNDED BY WATER. ALSO, THERE
ARE QUITE A FEW DESERT AREAS. THE ATMOSHPERE IS AN ODD GREEN COLOR,
AND THE GRASS USUALLY IS A FIREY ORANGE COLOR, BUT CAN VARY. DESERT
LAND USUALLY IS BLUE IN COLOR. SOMETIMES EVEN SOME ODD STRUCTURES
CAN BE SEEN, EVEN THOUGH THEY MAY BE MOUNTAINS, THEY RESEMBLE RUINS.
(NOTES BY JEFF KEMP EPOC 6011:630:111)
