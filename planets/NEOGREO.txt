Planet: NEOGREO
Starmap index: 07
Paris coordinates: 708048, 4444727, 37468260

Guide entries:
A VERY BRIGHT QUARTZ PLANET WITH 2 MOONS. SURFACE COLOR HAS SHADES OF
GREEN, AND THE BLACK SPOTS HOLD VERY BUMPY AND MOUNTAINOUS TERRAIN.
OTHERWISE, THIS PLANET IS AVERAGE IN LAND FEATURES.
